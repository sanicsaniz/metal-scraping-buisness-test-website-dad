About Dad Dad Dad Business

Started by three dads with trucks and big hearts, Dad Dad Dad Business helps local folks get rid of old appliances, junk metal, and e-waste — while keeping it out of landfills. We come to you, we pay you, and we keep it green.

Our mission is to make recycling easy, honest, and accessible for everyone in Miami-Dade and the surrounding areas. We believe in fair payouts, friendly service, and a cleaner community for our kids.
